Privacy

Privacy Notice

This notice explains how information you choose to send through the current static website is handled.

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Who is responsible for your information

Jinghang Global Supply Chain is the public-facing brand. Shenzhen Jinghang Freight Forwarding Co., Ltd. is the legal entity responsible for business enquiries covered by this notice. You can contact us at hello@jinghangsc.com.

What this website collects

The current website is static. It does not create a user account, take payment, run marketing analytics, or store contact-form entries in a website database.

If you use the project enquiry form, your browser asks your own email application to prepare a message. Nothing is sent until you choose to send that email. The information may include your name, work email, company, location, website, product and project details, supplier and cargo information, service needs, dates, and contact details.

Why we use enquiry information

We use the information to read and respond to your enquiry, assess project feasibility, clarify requirements, prepare a proposal or quotation, take requested steps before a possible contract, maintain relevant business records, and protect the security and integrity of our communications.

Where data-protection law requires a legal basis, the basis may be steps requested before entering a contract, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, or our legitimate interest in responding to and managing genuine business enquiries.

Service providers and international processing

Email providers, website hosting and security providers, and relevant professional or service providers may process information only as needed to deliver their services or help us respond to the enquiry. The website is hosted through Cloudflare Pages, and the public contact email currently uses Gmail, provided by Google. These providers may process technical or email data in more than one country under their own terms and safeguards.

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry information for as long as it is reasonably needed to respond, evaluate follow-up work, manage an active or potential business relationship, maintain necessary business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal or regulatory obligations. When those purposes no longer apply, information should be deleted or anonymized where reasonably practical.

Your choices and rights

You do not have to send an enquiry through this website. Where applicable law gives you data-protection rights, you may ask for access, correction, deletion, restriction, or a copy of your personal information, or object to certain processing. Some rights may be limited by legal or record-keeping requirements.

Send a request to hello@jinghangsc.com. We may need reasonable information to verify the request. You may also have the right to contact the data-protection authority where you live.

Cookies and website measurement

The current website source does not include advertising or marketing pixels, third-party analytics scripts, or consent-dependent cookies. Cloudflare may generate standard security, performance, or access records as the hosting provider. If optional analytics, advertising technology, or additional cookies are enabled later, this notice and any required consent controls should be updated first.

Security and external links

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the current website and email workflow, but no internet or email transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. Links to third-party websites are governed by those parties’ own notices and terms.

Updates to this notice

We may update this notice when the website, contact process, providers, or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.
