Company & fit

A China supply chain partner for overseas businesses

What does Jinghang Global Supply Chain do?

Jinghang Global Supply Chain is a China-based supply chain partner helping overseas businesses coordinate sourcing, supplier communication, quality inspection coordination, shipment consolidation, and cross-border logistics execution. Jinghang's own team and third-party providers are configured flexibly according to each project's needs. Related page: Connected Supply Chain Services.

Who does Jinghang Global Supply Chain support?

We support overseas small and mid-sized businesses, hardware startups, and companies that need China supply chain execution support. Our priority customer markets are the United States and Europe. Project fit and service scope are reviewed case by case. Related page: Hardware Startup Support.

Where is service coverage available in China?

Service coverage is available in Shenzhen. Yiwu and Qingdao are partner-warehouse collection and consolidation service points, not company-owned offices or warehouses. Related page: About Jinghang.

Sourcing & suppliers

Sourcing, evaluation support, communication, and sampling

Can you coordinate China sourcing?

Yes. We can coordinate supplier search from a written brief, compare potential suppliers using available public and supplier-provided information, organize quotations or samples, and provide an open-issues and next-actions list. The client gives final supplier approval in writing. Related page: China Sourcing Services.

Can you support supplier evaluation?

Yes. Preliminary supplier screening may compare requirement fit, available business and location information, supplier-provided capability information, quotation terms, MOQ, sample details, stated lead time, communication, and open risks. It is not a factory audit, certification, financial due diligence, or guarantee of supplier performance or product quality. Related page: China Sourcing Services.

Can you coordinate communication with suppliers we already use?

Yes. We can coordinate requirements, quotation or specification questions, samples, agreed follow-up, inspection preparation, collection, and delivery handoffs with existing suppliers. Responsibilities, decision points, and commercial terms are confirmed in writing for the project. Related page: China Sourcing Services.

Can you coordinate product samples?

Yes. Sampling coordination may include requested versions, quantities, accessories, packing information, supplier questions, and compatible sample consolidation within an agreed scope. Prototype, pilot, one-time, and small-batch requests are reviewed case by case. Related page: China Sourcing Services.

Inspection & consolidation

Pre-shipment checks and multi-supplier consolidation

Can you coordinate quality inspection before shipment?

Yes. Quality inspection can be coordinated before shipment. Routine agreed checks may be handled by Jinghang's team, while paid third-party professional or independent inspection can be coordinated when required. Method, sampling, criteria, evidence, provider, scope, and fee are confirmed before inspection. Related page: Quality Inspection Coordination.

What happens if an inspection finds issues?

The client decides whether to request rework, reinspection, or shipment release. Jinghang can communicate the agreed findings and coordinate supplier follow-up within the confirmed scope; additional work, timing, and fees are confirmed separately. Related page: Quality Inspection Coordination.

Can you consolidate shipments from multiple suppliers?

Yes. Compatible goods may be collected and consolidated after supplier timing, packaging, cargo characteristics, destination, customs requirements, storage, and release conditions are reviewed. Receiving checks, labeling, repacking, and other preparation work are included only when operationally available and agreed in writing. Related page: Freight From China.

DDP, DAP & routes

Service terms, quotation boundaries, and route review

Which international logistics methods can you coordinate?

We coordinate international express, air freight, and sea freight. The suitable option and final availability depend on the product, packed dimensions and weight, origin, destination, customs requirements, target timing, and route review. Related page: Freight From China.

Is DDP service available for the United States and Europe?

DDP is generally available for the United States and 25 verified European countries: Germany, France, Austria, Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, the Czech Republic, Poland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Denmark, Finland, Sweden, Hungary, Italy, Portugal, Spain, Bulgaria, Estonia, Greece, Croatia, Latvia, Lithuania, Romania, and Ireland. Every shipment remains subject to cargo, customs, destination, route, and partner review. DDP and DAP for Canada and Australia require actual-shipment feasibility and route review. Related page: DDP Shipping From China.

Is DAP service available for the United States and Europe?

Yes. DAP may be arranged globally, including for Canada and Australia, subject to cargo type, origin, destination, route, customs requirements, destination feasibility, and partner review. Availability, responsibilities, inclusions, exclusions, and pricing are confirmed for each shipment. Related page: DDP Shipping From China.

What is the difference between DDP and DAP in a Jinghang Global Supply Chain quotation?

Under the sales contract, DDP places import-clearance responsibility and applicable import duties and taxes on the seller, while DAP normally leaves import clearance and import duties or taxes to the buyer. Every quotation must still state the exact named place, parties, responsibilities, included charges, exclusions, and shipment conditions. Related page: DDP Shipping From China.

What charges will a DDP or DAP quotation define?

There is no universal fixed inclusion matrix. Each quotation states whether duties, VAT, customs clearance, remote-area fees, storage, redelivery, insurance, claim handling, and other destination charges are included or excluded, based on the actual cargo and project. Related page: DDP Shipping From China.

Do you offer DDU shipping?

We use DDP and DAP as the current public service terms. DDU is a legacy term, not a current Incoterms rule. If it appears in an enquiry, we clarify whether DDP or DAP is intended and confirm the named place, responsibilities, inclusions, and exclusions. Related page: DDP Shipping From China.

Which cross-border routes can you review?

Confirmed route examples include China to the United States, Europe, Hong Kong, and selected Southeast Asian markets; the United States to Europe, Hong Kong, and Mainland China; and Europe to the United States, Hong Kong, and Mainland China. Other feasible routes are reviewed case by case. Related page: Freight From China.

Can you coordinate logistics between origins outside China?

Yes. Selected multi-origin cross-border logistics may be coordinated on a project basis. Route availability depends on the cargo, origin, destination, customs requirements, destination feasibility, and partner-network review. Related page: Freight From China.

What determines whether a route is available?

Route availability depends on cargo type, documents, packaging, origin, destination, postcode, customs requirements, requested service, and carrier or partner-network review. We do not guarantee universal route availability, cargo acceptance, customs clearance, transit time, or delivery time. Related page: Freight From China.

Special cargo

Case-by-case cargo review and exclusions

Can you review battery-related, sensitive, or regulated cargo?

Yes. Battery-related, sensitive, and regulated cargo can be reviewed case by case. Acceptance depends on cargo characteristics, available documents, origin, destination, packaging, handling requirements, carrier or partner-network review, and customs requirements. There is no universal battery-document checklist or guaranteed acceptance. Related page: Freight and Special-Cargo Review.

Can you review oversized or overweight cargo?

Yes. Oversized and overweight cargo can be reviewed case by case. There is no universal fixed size or weight limit; acceptance depends on the actual dimensions, weight, packaging, handling requirements, origin, destination, route, and partner review. Related page: Freight and Special-Cargo Review.

Which goods will you not handle?

We do not handle prohibited goods, products restricted or banned from export by China, products restricted or banned from import by the destination country, counterfeit or infringing goods, or cargo without required compliance documents. Related page: Freight and Special-Cargo Review.
