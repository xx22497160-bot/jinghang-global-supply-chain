How we work

A typical project from brief to delivery.

Every engagement starts by defining the product, current stage, responsibilities, and known risks.

  1. 01

    Step 1: Receive the Brief

    Review the product, current stage, suppliers, quantity, destination, timeline, and known constraints.

  2. 02

    Step 2: Coordinate Suppliers & Samples

    Clarify requirements, follow up with factories, organize quotations or samples, and record client decisions.

  3. 03

    Step 3: Inspect & Correct

    Coordinate agreed checks, document findings, and follow supplier corrective actions before release.

  4. 04

    Step 4: Consolidate & Prepare

    Receive and verify cargo, then coordinate labeling, repacking, consolidation, and shipping documents as agreed.

  5. 05

    Step 5: Deliver & Review

    Coordinate international transport and final delivery, report exceptions, and carry useful lessons into the next order.

Fit matters

A partner for controlled growth—not shortcuts.

Clear boundaries protect the client, the cargo, and the long-term working relationship.

We are a strong fit when you need:

  • Reliable execution in China without a local procurement team
  • Supplier, sample, quality, consolidation, and delivery coordination
  • More visibility across several factories or service providers
  • A product-specific plan for complex cargo or fulfillment
  • A partner focused on clear, repeatable operations

We are not the right fit for:

  • Selection based only on the lowest standalone freight quote
  • Inaccurate declarations, duty avoidance, or non-compliant arrangements
  • Projects without enough product or shipment information to plan responsibly
  • Replacing qualified engineers, laboratories, certification bodies, or legal advisers
